Hello,

My name is BJ Ruffin, and I'd like to take a moment to tell you a little about myself. I am a hopeless romantic at heart — someone who has experienced the pain of love but refuses to let it diminish my belief in its power. Born and raised in Queens, New York, I have always been captivated by the magic of love and the resilience of the human spirit.

My mother is the source of my inspiration. She loved romance novels and inspired me to pursue writing later in life. I cared for her until she passed away in 2017, and I still feel her presence with me every day.

One of my greatest passions is spending time at the beach, where I find solace in the rhythmic waves and the vast expanse of the ocean. The beach is my sanctuary — a place where I can reflect, dream, and draw inspiration for my writing. The warm sand between my toes and the salty breeze on my skin rejuvenates my spirit and reminds me of the beauty in the world.

"Love is worth pursuing despite the scars it may leave behind."

Through my writing, I aim to capture the essence of love — the joy, the heartbreak, and the hope it brings. I believe in the power of words, and I strive to create stories that touch readers' hearts, reminding them that love is worth pursuing despite the scars it may leave behind. My journey as an author is an exploration of the human experience — an endeavor to inspire others to embrace love and all its complexities.

Together, let's discover the power of love one story at a time. I hope you enjoy reading these stories just as much as I enjoy writing them.

A note on privacy

Why I write faceless.

The books are the public part of me. My family and quiet life aren't. You'll meet me on the page — through the characters, the small towns, the porches, and the people learning to love each other a little better. That's the version of me worth knowing.
